Stay Safe Online With Aunties

Your security is our priority!

1. Protect Your Password

Your password is the gateway to your account. Follow these guidelines to keep your password secure:

  • Create a unique password (never reuse passwords across websites)
  • On SignUp, avoid common and weak passwords (e.g., "123456", "password")
  • Use a mix of upper and lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters (e.g., CassPlaR59#Fy)
  • Change your password periodically (At least every 2 months)

2. Beware of Phishing Scams

We will never ask for your login information via email, phone, or text. Be cautious when receiving unsolicited communications.

  • Check for signs of suspicious emails (poor grammar, strange sender addresses, etc.)
  • Verify any request through our official contact channels before acting

3. Payment Security

When making payments, ensure your information is protected:

  • Never share your full bank account details
  • Only provide payment details through trusted, secure methods
  • Always verify that you're paying to the correct account

4. Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)

We highly recommend enabling two-factor authentication (2FA) on your account to add an extra layer of security. This ensures that even if someone learns your password, they won’t be able to access your account without the second verification step.

5. Respect Your Privacy

We respect your privacy and commit to safeguarding your personal information. Be mindful when using shared devices.

6. Stay Alert

Regularly monitor your account activity for any suspicious actions and report them immediately.

  • Keep track of your orders and payments
  • If something seems off, report it to us right away

Important Reminder

We will never ask for your login details or financial information over email, phone, or SMS. If you receive any suspicious messages, do not respond. Instead, contact us directly through our website.

What to Do If You Suspect a Scam

If you receive a suspicious message or phone call, follow these steps:

  • Do not click any links or open attachments
  • Delete the message immediately
  • Verify any claims with Auntie directly
  • Report the issue through our support channels
