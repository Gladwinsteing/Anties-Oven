Password manager:

A tool that securely stores and manages your passwords.

Two-factor authentication (2FA):

An extra layer of security requiring two forms of identification.

Email or SMS OTP:

One-time passwords sent via SMS for authentication.

Phishing:

A method of trying to gather personal information using deceptive emails and websites.

Social Media Privacy:

Protecting your personal information on social media platforms.

Hacking:

Unauthorized access to data in a system or computer.

Screen Lock:

A security feature that locks your device screen.

VPN:

Virtual Private Network, which encrypts your internet connection for privacy.

Ad-blocker:

Applications that block unwanted ads on websites.

HTTP and HTTPS:

Protocols for transferring data on the web, with HTTPS being secure.

Wi-Fi Network:

A wireless network for connecting devices to the internet.

Email Spam Filter:

A tool that filters out unwanted email.

Antivirus Software:

Software designed to detect and destroy computer viruses.

Cyberbullying:

Bullying that takes place over digital devices.
